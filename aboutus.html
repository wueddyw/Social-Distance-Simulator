Social Distance Warriors

Greetings! The Social Distance Warriors present you with their game development debut: SOCIAL DISTANCING SIMULATOR!

Social Distancing Simulator tackles one of the greatest challenged brought upon us by COVID 19. Grocery Shopping! Navigate your character through a grocery store and collect all the ingredients on your list. Be careful though! Being too close to other shoppers will increase your risk of catching the virus, so stay out of their way!

Our teams objective for this project was to present our audience with a fun, engaging pastime during the social distancing times of COVID 19. Our world these days has plenty of doom and gloom. Our team believes that it is important to keep things light, even during these scary times. This ideology is reflected well in our team culture. We consistently strive to enjoy our work, while also recognizing the gravity of the problems facing the world.

Enjoy the game!
